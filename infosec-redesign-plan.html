1 All Changes — From First to Last
🟢

STEP 1 — Disable "Coming Soon" Mode

Go to WordPress Admin → Settings → Site Visibility → Set to Public. This makes the real site visible to everyone.

5 minutes
🧭

STEP 2 — Fix the Navigation Menu

Update the top navigation bar to have: Home | About | Blog | Tools | Contact. Make it sticky (stays at top when scrolling). Update colours to match dark navy theme.

30 minutes
🦸

STEP 3 — Redesign the Homepage Hero Section

Replace the current generic hero with a bold statement: Name + Title + "Securing Digital Solutions" tagline. Add two buttons: "View My Work" and "Contact Me". Use dark navy background with gold text accents.

1–2 hours
👤

STEP 4 — Add a "Who I Am" Summary Section (Homepage)

Short 3-line bio on the homepage: Director of Technology Services | 12+ Years | Africa & Middle East. Link to full About page. This is the executive summary that grabs attention fast.

45 minutes
🏅

STEP 5 — Create a "Hall of Fame" Logos Section (Homepage)

Show all the company logos (Microsoft, BBC, Oracle, Dell, Nokia, Siemens, UN, JP Morgan, EU CERT, etc.) in a clean horizontal scrolling or grid layout with the heading: "Recognised By The World's Top Organisations". This is his #1 credibility signal.

1 hour
🛠️

STEP 6 — Add a "My Tools" Section (Homepage)

Show his 3 published GitHub tools as cards: FortiGate Audit Tool, Cisco Audit Tool, Password Audit Tool. Each card has: tool name, 1-line description, and a "View on GitHub" button. This shows he's an active builder, not just a talker.

1 hour
📰

STEP 7 — Add "Latest Articles" Preview Section (Homepage)

Show the 3 most recent blog posts in a card layout. Title, short excerpt, and "Read More" button. Pull directly from existing posts — no new content needed.

45 minutes
📬

STEP 8 — Add a Contact / Hire Me Section (Homepage)

Simple section at the bottom: "Want to work with me?" + email link or button. Clean, dark background, gold CTA button.

30 minutes
📄

STEP 9 — Redesign the About Me Page

Break the wall of plain text into visual sections: Career Timeline (PwC → KPMG → Standard Chartered → KCB → Director), Key Achievements cards, and the Hall of Fame logo grid moved here in full. Add a "First Bugcrowd + HackerOne Ambassador from Africa" highlight.

1.5 hours
🎨

STEP 10 — Update Fonts and Colours Site-Wide

Change body font to Inter or Roboto (already available in WordPress). Ensure consistent use of: Navy #060f29, Gold #CAAB57, White #F2F2F2. Remove any leftover template colours.

45 minutes
🦶

STEP 11 — Update the Footer

Add: Name, tagline ("Securing Digital Solutions"), navigation links, social media icons (LinkedIn, GitHub, Twitter). Copyright line at the bottom. Currently the footer is probably the template default.

30 minutes
📱

STEP 12 — Mobile Check and Fixes

Test every page on a phone screen. Fix any sections that are too wide, text too small, or buttons that don't work on touch. Elementor has a mobile editor built in — use it to adjust spacing and font sizes for mobile.

1 hour
🚀

STEP 13 — Final Review + Handover

Walk through the full site one more time. Check all links work. Confirm Coming Soon is OFF. Share the final live URL with the client.

30 minutes
